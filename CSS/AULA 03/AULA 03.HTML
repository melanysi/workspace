Termo Afropaty

Este termo é utilizado para denominar mulheres negras com alto poder aquisitivo que têm um estilo de vida de “patricinhas”, ou seja, que amam ostentar roupas de grife, joias e viagens para o exterior termo este que começou a ganhar forças nos dias atuais.

inspirações de Preta Patricia

Hilary Banks ( Um Maluco no Pedaço/ Fresh Prince of Bel-Air)
Hilary is the oldest daughter of the Banks family. In the earlier seasons, Hilary's snobbishness is emphasized, as well as her shallow trendy environmental activism. She is best described as impulsive, shallow, attractive, most of the time very dim-witted and extremely self-centered, who can come off as a very arrogant airhead; she looks down on Will's upbringing and is obsessed with shopping and money. However, despite her apparent shallow personality, deep down she has a strong sense of integrity, and shows obvious affection for her family members. She is often portrayed as having no common sense.
